About Us

Meet the team behind Sohome Marine Transport & Logistics

Our Journey

Founded in Kolkata, India, Sohome Marine Transport & Logistics has established itself as a leading provider of marine-based transport and logistics solutions. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have successfully navigated the challenging waters of global shipping and logistics.

Our team of experienced professionals brings together expertise from various aspects of the maritime and logistics industry, enabling us to offer comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and sustainable marine transport and logistics solutions that connect businesses globally while maintaining the highest standards of safety, service, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for marine transport and logistics services worldwide, recognized for our innovation, reliability, and commitment to excellence.
