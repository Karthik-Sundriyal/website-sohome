ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded in 2010, JellyFish Adventures began as a small family business on the beautiful shores of Calicut, Kerala. What started as a passion for water sports quickly evolved into a mission to share the thrill and beauty of coastal adventures with everyone.

Today, our team of certified instructors and adventure enthusiasts is dedicated to providing safe, exciting, and unforgettable experiences for adventurers of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned pros.

Our Mission

At JellyFish Adventures, we're committed to:

  • Promoting water sports and adventure in Kerala's beautiful coastal waters
  • Providing world-class equipment and expert instruction
  • Ensuring safety while delivering exhilarating experiences
  • Supporting local communities and environmental conservation
  • Making adventure accessible to all ages and abilities

Why Choose Us?

With our prime location in Calicut, expert instructors, state-of-the-art equipment, and a perfect safety record, JellyFish Adventures offers the ultimate water sports experience. Whether you're a thrill-seeker looking for your next adventure or a family wanting to try something new together, we have the perfect activity for you.
